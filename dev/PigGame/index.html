GAME RULES:

Score points by rolling the dice. Fist player to 100 wins.

If you roll a one, you lose your turn. If double sixes are rolled twice in a row, you lose all your points and your turn.

You can keep your current score and pass by clicking hold.

You can set the winning score to something different in the box.
